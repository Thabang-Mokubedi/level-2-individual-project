University of Johannesburg

Events Management Website built using WordPress and Advanced Custom Fields.

The Institute for the Future of Knowledge is a cross-disciplinary ecosystem at the University of Johannesburg, devoted to understanding the relationship between what we know and what will happen, for the purpose of improving both, for everyone.

The client had UI / UX Figma designs done by another agency and required us to bring them to life on a live WordPress website.
The site admin has the ability to upload events on the website where users can register and receive calendar notifications days before the event.

myCAhub

SAAS Web Application with a Google Chrome Extension built with React, NodeJS, Express & MongoDB.

MyCAhub™ provides simple software solutions to problems faced by Chartered Accountants in South Africa, as well as curated weekly content centred around personal finance.

Their first product, The Reflective Plan Toolkit™, helps CA(SA)s complete their annual CPD reflective plans as per SAICA requirements. Users simply select their Career Path and Proficiency Level, then the software will automatically: a) Determine the user’s specific competencies and required level per competency b) Help the user assess their competencies to identify relevant learning areas for the year.

Vayakos

Android & iOS Food Delivery Mobile Apps for South African Restaurants and Drivers, similar to Uber Eats.

Vayakos is South Africa’s very own local food delivery app which gives users access to township and urban food. Their application is similar to popular food delivery apps such as Uber Eats & Mr. Delivery, but they specifically serve the African Food niche.

  • A client app used by the general public to view restaurant menus and make orders.
  • A driver app used by drivers to manage their deliveries.
  • A restaurant app used by store owners to manage their menu items and accept or decline orders.
  • An admin web app used by Vayakos to oversee everything on the platform.

Chartered Institute of Procurement & Supply (CIPS)

Learning Management System & E-Commerce Application for an International Business School built using WordPress and PHP.

The Chartered Institute of Procurement & Supply (CIPS) is a United Kingdom-based global Professional Body working for Purchasing and Supply Professions. In 2019, they partnered with a South African College, ISAF Africa, to deliver CIPS Professional Certificates & Diplomas to the African continent.

In order to reach the entire continent, the institution required a website that enabled students to take courses, exams & group discussions online. They also required E-Commerce functionality which allows students to purchase courses directly from the website using an online payment gateway.

Melsoft also carried out the entire UI / UX design of the website, the company’s branding as well as running profitable Social Media adverts.
